The Problem

Juber had 250+ rego papers in a filing cabinet, zero visibility of which vehicles were overdue, and was chasing customer payments manually every single day. Vehicles were being serviced without any history tracked — meaning the same problems kept coming back.

RunSheet

Sydney Dispatch · Delivery operations platform

The Problem

Dispatchers had zero visibility of where drivers actually were. Run coordination happened entirely over WhatsApp. Proof of delivery was a phone call. Delays weren't caught until customers were already angry.

What We Built

  • Live map showing every driver's location and status in real time
  • Digital run sheets replacing paper manifests entirely
  • In-app proof of delivery — photo capture on job completion
  • Dispatch hub with automatic delay detection and alerts
  • Customer notifications sent automatically when runs fall behind
  • Weekly performance reports per driver

"Built so dispatchers see everything before it becomes a problem — drivers do less admin, and customers stop calling to ask where their delivery is."

Currently live. Performance data published after 6 months of production.
