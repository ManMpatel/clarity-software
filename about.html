How we work

Simple process. No surprises.

You shouldn't need a project manager to hire a software company. Here's exactly how it goes from first conversation to live system.

01

We come to you

We travel to your Sydney workplace — your warehouse, production floor, or office. We sit with you and watch how your team actually runs the business. No tech jargon. No slide decks. Just a real conversation about what's slowing you down and where the problems are.

02

Free interactive prototype — before you pay anything

We build a fully clickable mockup of your system before you sign anything. Real buttons. Real screen layouts. Real data flows. You get to test it, walk through it with your team, and decide if it's exactly right. This costs you nothing and obligates you to nothing.

03

Fixed price quote — one number, no surprises

If the prototype is right, we send a written scope and a single fixed price. No hourly billing. No "it depends." No change requests that arrive as surprise invoices. One number, agreed upfront, that covers the full build.

04

We build it — weekly demos, no black boxes

4–8 weeks typically. We demo the real system to you every week so your team can test it as we go. You're never waiting months only to find out something is wrong. Feedback is instant, changes are immediate.

05

Launch day — on-site, together

We come on-site for launch. We train your team on the real system, not a recording. We handle the hosting, the deployment, the DNS. Once it's live, we stay on a $250/month retainer — handling support, bug fixes, small changes, and keeping it running as you grow.
