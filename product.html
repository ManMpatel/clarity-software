What is Clarity Fleet

Two things. One system.

Clarity Fleet is a custom web dashboard paired with a small GPS device that sits in each vehicle. The device sends live data over 4G — location, engine health, speed, fuel level — straight to your dashboard. No driver apps. No logging in. It works the moment the ignition turns on.

Setup process

Three steps. Your whole fleet is live.

No IT department needed. No training courses. We handle the entire setup, then hand it to you.

hardware
1

Hardware installation

An auto electrician installs the GPS device behind each vehicle's dashboard. Takes 30–45 minutes per vehicle. Once it's in, it's tamper-proof — drivers can't unplug it.

cloud_sync
2

Data flows live

The device connects via 4G (Telstra or Optus) and starts sending data the moment the ignition turns on. Location, speed, engine health, fuel — arriving in real time.

dashboard
3

You see everything

Open the dashboard on any phone, tablet, or computer. Every vehicle, every alert, every payment — updated live. We train your team on day one and stay on support.

Optional add-ons

Extra hardware for specialist fleets.

Can be added any time after the base system is live. Extra hardware cost applies per vehicle.

thermostat

Temperature probes

Live temperature inside cargo compartments. Up to 4 probes per vehicle. Dashboard alerts when temperature goes out of your set range.

Suited to: Refrigerated vans, food delivery, pharmaceutical transport
door_sensor

Door open/close sensor

Records every time a cargo door opens or closes — time-stamped with location. Alerts if a door is left open while the vehicle is moving.

Suited to: Delivery vans, proof-of-delivery workflows, secure transport
badge

Driver identification

A key fob reader inside the cab. Each driver taps their fob before driving. System logs exactly who drove each vehicle for every trip — automatic.

Suited to: Shared fleets, multiple drivers per vehicle, compliance-heavy industries
