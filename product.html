Setup process

Three steps. Your whole fleet is live.

No IT department needed. No training courses. We handle the entire setup on-site and hand it to you running.

01

Hardware installation

An auto electrician installs the GPS device behind each vehicle's dashboard. 30–45 minutes per vehicle. Once it's in, it's tamper-proof — drivers can't unplug it.

02

Data flows live

The device connects via 4G (Telstra or Optus) and starts sending data the moment the ignition turns on. Location, speed, engine health, fuel — arriving in real time.

03

You see everything

Open the dashboard on any phone, tablet, or laptop. Every vehicle, every alert, every payment — updated live. We train your team on day one and stay on support.

What it does

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Built around how fleet operators actually run their day — not how software companies think they should.

Live fleet map

Every vehicle on a live map. Colour-coded status — moving, idling, stopped, off. See your entire fleet at a glance from any device, right now.

Rego & service alerts

Automated alerts for rego renewals, upcoming services by odometer, and engine fault codes. Know before the problem hits.

Staff check-ins

GPS-verified clock-ins. See who started, where they were when they checked in, and how long they've been out. No more calling drivers.

Auto-debit payments

PayWay BECS direct debit built in. Weekly or fortnightly rental payments collected automatically. Funds in your account — no chasing required.

Full service history

Every service, fault, and repair logged per vehicle. Know what's been done on every vehicle, by who, and when — planned by real odometer reading.

WhatsApp notifications

Automatic WhatsApp messages to customers — payment confirmations, rego reminders, alerts. Sent via Meta Business API from your number.

Optional add-ons

Extra hardware for specialist fleets.

Added any time after the base system is live. Extra hardware cost per vehicle applies.

Temperature probes

Live temperature inside cargo compartments. Up to 4 probes per vehicle. Dashboard alerts when temperature goes out of your set range.

Suited to: Refrigerated vans, food delivery, pharmaceutical transport

Door open/close sensor

Records every time a cargo door opens or closes — time-stamped with location. Alerts if a door is left open while the vehicle is moving.

Suited to: Delivery vans, proof-of-delivery workflows, secure transport

Driver identification

A key fob reader inside the cab. Each driver taps their fob before driving. System logs exactly who drove each vehicle for every trip — automatic.

Suited to: Shared fleets, multiple drivers per vehicle, compliance requirements
