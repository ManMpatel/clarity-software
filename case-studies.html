one dashboard.

Scooter & car rental · Auburn, Sydney NSW · Owner: Juber · Live since 2024

Before
250+ rego papers in filing cabinets. Chasing payments on WhatsApp. No service history on any vehicle.
→
After
One dashboard. Auto-debit payments. Rego alerts. GPS staff check-ins. 15 hours saved every week.

Juber came to us managing one of Sydney's busiest scooter and car rental operations entirely on paper. Rego renewal dates were tracked in a filing cabinet. Staff check-ins happened over the phone. Customers were chased for overdue payments manually, one by one on WhatsApp. Vehicle service history didn't exist — meaning the same mechanical faults kept coming back and costing more each time.

We built FleetAI — a full fleet operations platform built specifically around how Dasi Boys runs. Every vehicle tracked live via GPS. Rego alerts automated. Payments collected via PayWay BECS direct debit. Staff clock-ins verified by location. Service history logged per vehicle, planned by real odometer reading.

Coast Bay —
From hours per quote
to minutes.

Custom cabinetry manufacturer · Sydney NSW · Multi-builder production suite · Live since 2024

Before
Quoting took hours. Stock not tracked. Design changes lost between office and factory. Expensive rework on every project.
→
After
10+ quotes before lunch. 94% on-time delivery. Zero stock orders lost. Builder-by-builder tracking with stage checklists.

This Sydney cabinet manufacturer was running a complex multi-builder operation almost entirely on manual processes. Quotes took hours to produce. Stock levels weren't tracked — so the wrong materials kept getting ordered, causing expensive rework. Design changes discussed in the office never made it to the factory floor reliably. And with no visibility into which builder was on which stage of which job, nobody knew what was actually on-time until it was late.

We built a full production suite that covers the entire workflow from first quote to final delivery — quoting with automatic BOM sheets, live stock management, designer workload tracking, and a builder-by-builder progress board with locking stage checklists that prevent any job moving forward until every step is signed off.
